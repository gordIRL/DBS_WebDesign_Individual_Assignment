Frequently Asked Questions

General / Check In etc:

  • Regular check-in times are daily from 12pm - 6pm.
  • Please make the B&B aware if you plan to arrive outside of these hours to arrange.
  • Free 24-hour wireless broadband Internet access throughout.
  • Free private off street car park.
  • All taxes are included in rate.
  • Please note all rooms are strictly non-smoking!
  • All rooom have private bathrooms.
  • Credit Cards: Visa, Mastercard - accepted.
  • Gift Vouchers are available.
  • Open all year round.
  • Full breakfast is served from 7.30am weekdays and from 7am weekends.

Delicious Breakfasts:

Winner of ‘Best Breakfast Award’ Breakfast, in the bright leafy Conservatory, is the only meal served at Ellens's B&B. Everything is freshly prepared and cooked to order. We pride ourselves on our breakfast with a varied choice of dishes including seafood, devilled kidneys, the ‘full Irish’, locally-smoked salmon, organic porridge, free-range eggs and farm cheeses. Our famous brown bread and fruit brack are both baked daily in the AGA. We only serve freshly-squeezed orange juice together with fresh fruits, organic muesli and yogurt. All special dietary requirements can be looked after.
